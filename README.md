# CV Tailor 📄✨

CV Tailor is a full-stack app to automatically format, rewrite, and tailor their resumes and cover letters for specific job descriptions. By leveraging Google's Gemini AI, the app analyzes your base resume against a target job description, extracts missing keywords, rewrites your professional summary and experience bullets, and drafts a cover letter.

## Features

- **Base Resume Management**: Store your master resumes with your skills and detailed experience text.
- **AI-Powered Tailoring**: Uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to intelligently extract missing keywords from the job description and rewrite your resume's summary and top bullet points to perfectly align with the target role.
- **Automatic Cover Letters**: Generates a professional, engaging 3-paragraph cover letter tailored to the specific company and role.
- **Interactive Dashboard**: A modern, glassmorphism-themed Next.js frontend to manage base resumes, generate new applications, and view your tailored assets.
